Questions founders ask

Clear answers.
No theatre.

A good partnership starts with the right expectations.

Can you guarantee sales?

No. Nobody serious can guarantee a commercial outcome from creator marketing. We build the strategy, creator fit and measurement plan that give a campaign its best chance to work—and report honestly on what happens.

How do you choose creators?

We evaluate audience relevance, content quality, authenticity, commercial fit and working style. The shortlist comes with the reasoning—not just names and follower counts.

Do you negotiate creator pricing?

Yes. We discuss deliverables, usage rights, timelines and commercial terms so the agreement is clear and appropriate for the campaign.

What does reporting include?

A campaign summary, content links, available performance data, learnings and practical recommendations for the next campaign.
